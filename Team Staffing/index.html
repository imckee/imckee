about us

Headquartered in Muscatine, IA, Team Staffing Solutions is trusted by leading businesses and industries throughout the state of Iowa. Team Staffing Solutions is committed to being the highest quality supplier of employment solutions. Our services are delivered with a sense of mutual respect, integrity, and honesty, as well as the appreciation for the growth of our Clients and Team Members. Our experienced account managers will become a vital extension of your Human Resources Department and are dedicated to strengthening your workforce with qualified candidates in the areas of light industrial, clerical and professional positions. We provide training, education and evaluation to ensure that highly skilled employees are placed in you company.
